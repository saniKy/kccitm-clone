Careers & Mentorship

Careers

Building successful careers is a shared journey—and our alumni play a vital role in guiding the next generation. This section connects students and alumni through career opportunities, mentorship, and real-world exposure, creating a powerful ecosystem of growth and support.

Jobs by Alumni

Discover job opportunities shared directly by our alumni across leading organizations and industries. These openings offer trusted referrals, insider insights, and a pathway to career advancement through the alumni network.

Mentorship Program

Learn, grow, and get guided by those who have walked the path before you.

  • Become a Mentor: Give back by sharing your experience, industry knowledge, and career guidance with aspiring students.
  • Find a Mentor: Connect with experienced alumni who can help you navigate career decisions, skills development, and professional growth.

Internships & Referrals

Designed especially for final-year students, this initiative provides access to internships and referral opportunities shared by alumni, helping students gain hands-on experience and improve their employability.

Ramveer Tanwar

Ramveer Tanwar, popularly known as The Pond Man of India, is a proud alumnus and an inspiring environmentalist dedicated to water conservation and pond revival across India. Through his grassroots efforts and community-led initiatives, he has played a significant role in restoring hundreds of traditional water bodies, creating sustainable impact at the local and national levels. His remarkable work has earned recognition at the highest level and has been mentioned by Hon’ble Prime Minister Narendra Modi, underscoring the national importance of his mission. As an alumnus, Ramveer Tanwar stands as a powerful example of leadership, social responsibility, and purpose-driven action, inspiring students to contribute meaningfully to society.
