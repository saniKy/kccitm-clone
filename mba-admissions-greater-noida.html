MBA Plus

Course Overview

Apply Online
KCC Institute offers a full-time MBA Plus program that lasts two years, divided into four semesters, which students can join with or without an entrance exam. The curriculum ensures consistency and high standards of education in Business and management studies as per the format prescribed by AKTU.
 
An MBA Plus degree aids in developing a solid theoretical base in business principles. It also develops a variety of soft skills, like negotiation, motivation, and communication, as well as hard skills in management and leadership roles.
 
KCC ITM’s flagship MBA Plus program is more than just a PG degree; a highly application-centric, industry-integrated program that balances theory and practice with a structure you can tailor to meet your leadership and development needs. The program comes in a range of specializations depending on your career obligations, with a focus on real-world practices, communication skills, and other soft skills.
 
A robust curriculum led by exceptional faculties and approved by AKTU. Becoming an MBA Plus student at KCCITM means learning from the best in the industry, which propels lifelong growth and a rapid acceleration in your career path.
 
The first year will give you a rigorous and analytical foundation that follows advanced training in core subjects, practical classes, and electives. The second year is full of case studies, live projects, and competitions, which is an excellent way to hone your presentation and communication skills. No matter what your end goal is, our MBA Plus program will help you come to be a stronger leader and a better person.
 
Our MBA Plus program is aimed at producing smart leaders who are quick in decision-making and do not flinch from taking bold steps for the greater good of the business community. We teach business ethics and moral duties to encourage students to take responsibility and contribute to nation-building at large.

MBA Plus Eligibility
 
To be eligible for MBA Plus admission at the best MBA college in Greater Noida, keep the following points in mind.
 
  • Graduate students from any stream having a minimum of 50% (or 45% for SC/ST) are eligible to apply for admission to the MBA Plus program at KCC ITM. 
  • Students in their final year of graduation are also eligible for admission provisionally till they receive their final graduation certificates and meet the eligibility criteria.
  • They must possess a graduate degree from any university accredited by the UGC.

Experiential Learning

Students in experiential learning environments collaborate and learn from one another in a more semi-structured manner, in contrast to traditional classroom settings, where students may compete with one another or remain disengaged or unmotivated, and where the education is highly structured. The goal of our MBA Plus is to include students in hands-on activities that are connected to real-world issues and circumstances, where the teacher supports rather than guides the students' development. KCC ITM places more emphasis on the learning process than the final product.

Admission Eligibility & Process

FAQ

Q1. What is the MBA Plus admission process at KCC ITM?
A bachelor's degree from any recognized college or institution with a minimum aggregate percentage of 50% is required for admission to the MBA Plus program at KCC ITM. Following the counseling and Personal Interview, candidates will be chosen on the basis of their merits.
 
Q2. How many MBA Plus specializations are available through KCC ITM?
Marketing, Finance, Human Resources and Organizational Behavior, International Business, Operations Management, and Information Technology are the six specializations available in the full-time MBA Plus program at KCC ITM College.
 
Q3. Is a part-time MBA Plus offered at KCC ITM University?
NO, the KCC ITM in Greater Noida does not offer an MBA Plus program in part-time mode. 
 
Q4. Can I apply for direct admission in MBA Plus at KCC ITM?
Yes, any graduate can apply for direct admission based on merit, but they have to satisfy the basic eligibility criteria.
 
Q5. Is KCC ITM’s MBA Plus program accredited?
Yes, KCC ITM’s MBA Plus program is accredited by AICTE. The syllabus and marking system are set as per the standards of AKTU.
