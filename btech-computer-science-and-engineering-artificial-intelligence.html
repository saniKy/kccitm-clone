B.Tech. in Computer Science & Engineering (Artificial Intelligence)

Course Overview

Apply Online

The B Tech Computer Science and Engineering with Artificial Intelligence program offers aspiring engineers an amazing selection of courses centered around the cutting edge of Artificial Intelligence (AI) with a foundation in Computer Science and Engineering. The four-year, full-time curriculum exposes students to practical aspects of technology to develop applications and solutions for the society we live in.

A career in  B. tech artificial intelligence (AI), such as that of a business intelligence developer, big data engineer, research scientist, data scientist, machine learning engineer, AI data analyst, AI engineer, robotics scientist, etc., can be very promising given the huge expansion of data and its uses.

Reputable companies including Microsoft, Amazon, Goldman Sachs, Oracle GBU, Cisco, Dell Technologies, Accenture, and others have hired students with specialized job descriptions on B. Tech CSE with artificial intelligence.

Students learn practical skills through hi-tech labs, and modern research facilities, equipped with prototypes and samples of complex machinery and computer systems. Internships and industry visits are also provided to give students a quick glance at corporate work culture.    

AI will be the basis for many future employment.

The program's graduates have excellent research prospects and can pursue higher education and research at prestigious national or foreign universities.

The average salary for graduates of the B. Tech Computer Science and Engineering with a focus on Artificial Intelligence can range from INR 8 LPA to 20 LPA, which can rise to INR 50 LPA as they gain experience. 

 

Major Highlights

The curriculum for computer science engineering maintains up with the changing demands of the business and advances in fields like artificial intelligence, machine learning, data science, cloud computing, robotics, and cyber security, which improves opportunities for postsecondary education and interdisciplinary research.

During the B.Tech transition to professional competency development activities, obtain more than fifteen distinct certifications in life skills from industry partners.

The Department of CSE in AI provides a dynamic environment for students to develop into tomorrow's sustainable engineers by providing state-of-the-art facilities, a center of excellence, highly skilled teachers, and great industry-academic interactions.

One of Greater Noida’s top engineering colleges, the Department of Computer Science Engineering offers top-notch internship opportunities.

Who ought to select CSE with AI?

The pupils who are particularly interested in:

  • Mathematical Reasoning & Programming
  • Computer programming
  • Robotics
  • Machine Learning
  • Automation
  • Deep Learning
  • AI Tools like ChatGPT, Fliki, Replit, Writesonic, etc….
Eligibility of B. Tech Computer Science Engineering College in Greater Noida, Delhi NCR
Candidate must have passed 10+2 examination with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects along with one of the following subjects: Chemistry / Biotechnology / Biology.
Admission Procedure - BTech CSE College in Greater Noida
Admission to the B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering would be done on the basis of JEE Mains 2026 & 10+2. Management seats are filled directly by the institute by conducting institute level counseling.
NRI Seats
5% of the management seats would be available for NRI candidates. NRI seats would be filled up as per AICTE and University norms i.e. by NRIs who have passed the qualifying examination with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics from an institute situated outside India provided such qualification is considered equivalent to 10+2 Examination by the Association of Indian Universities.

Scholarships and Fee Structure (By Centre & State Government)

  • Minority (MOMA) Scholarship
  • J&K Scholarships
  • North Eastern Region Scholarships
  • Scholarships for the Students with Disabilities

Complete details about the scholarships by Centre & State Government can be obtained from the admission office of the institute or social welfare officer of the concerned State Government.

Scholarship based on class XIIth score
  • 90% and above in eligiblity subjects - 100% of semester fee
  • 80%-89% in eligiblity subjects - 50% of semester fee.
  • 75%-79% in eligiblity subjects - 25% of semester fee
  • 70%-74% in eligiblity subjects - 15% of semester fee

* This scholastic scholarship is granted only in the first semester

Sports Scholarship
  • Zonal Zonal Level Player : 5% of fee each year
  • State State Level Player : 15% of fee each year
  • National National Level Player : 25% of fee each year
Defence Scholarship

Children of defence personnel's are ofered scholarship to the extent of 10% of fee each semester. The student has to produce card of his parent at the time of admission as a proof of service. The scholarship is also offered to wards of Ex-servicemen.
