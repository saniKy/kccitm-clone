Greetings, students !

Societies and clubs

  • THESPIAN-Theatre Society: Theatre society of KCCILHE is comprised of students who are passionate about theatre. The society works to sensitise people of the societal, environmental, physical, mental etc. realities of the societies, regions, cultures we are part of by giving ‘special performances of plays which have a literary, artistic, cultural and social rather than a commercial value’ attached to it. Theatre society brings out hidden talents in the students, provides a stage to scriptwriters, musicians, artists and anyone who is ready to contribute and participate in the society.
  • GLITZ-Fashion Society: Fashion Society of KCCILHE ensures that the students who become part of this society must get a chance to enhance their skills for modelling, styling, photography, film, event planning and advertising. The society aims to develop students’ interest and give them a greater perspective on the ins and outs of the fashion industry and fashion trends as well as giving them an outlet for their creativity. The society lays a platform where students can celebrate all forms of fashion.
  • SHUTTERBUGS-Photography Society: Photography society of KCCILHE is a platform where students can explore the art of clicking. The society aims to bring inspiration, creativity and connection through photography, as it makes sure that a photographer sees what a common man can envision. It brings together like-minded people and shares knowledge about the art and science of Photography. It encourages students to study light and impart tips on composition.
  • MELOMANIA-Music Society: Music Society aims to be a catalyst, ensuring that everyone across the campus has access to and opportunities for active music-making in their scholastic environment. It appreciates and promotes  all forms of music be it classical, pop, western raps, etc. and supports first-hand training for musical instruments like guitar, piano, drums. The society not only uplift the individual performers but also emphasizes on the training of ensemble playing, aural development and musical appreciation. 
  • BIBLIOPHILE-Literary Society: Literary Society aims to develop an environment of reading, writing and discussion to develop presentation and creativity skills of its members. It aims to make one platform where all can share their feelings about surroundings, their insights on politics, life, and college issues, their frustrations, trials, and happy moments in a creative way. This society is a community made for people who love literature and want to share their experiences, readings and thoughts with others who have the same interest and talent.
  • SPORTSVILLA-Sports Society SPORTS SOCIETY is a community to encourage sports activities in the KCCILHE. The aim is to bring talents together; encourage and support them to participate in Institutional, State and National Level Sports. The Sports Society aims to foster healthy sporting habits by instilling values in an adequate learning environment with opportunities for all students to play recreationally and competitively whilst maintaining a safe, friendly and equitable environment.
  • SWAY-Dance Society Dance Society of KCCILHE is comprised of fervent dancers who not only engage in technique-based training but also learn the finer nuances like articulating and breathing from the soul and inspire self through the medium of dance. The society encompasses all forms of dance such as classical choreography which includes jazz, contemporary, ballet and western which comprises hip-hop, street & break dance; wherein synergised dance renditions are prepared. Furthermore, all its members are inspired and given a chance to grow as solo performers.
  • TECHSTARS-Technical Society The Society aims to promote the interest of science and technology amongst students of KCCILHE. The society aims to assist and contribute to the production and development of good quality technicians needed by the reputed world organizations. The society tries to impart knowledge and first-hand training from various fields of science, technology and related areas, in order to foster research, educational processes and changing conditions & technology. The society will bring about effective linkage between industry, technical institutions and students.
  • ART ATTACK- Art & Craft Society: ART ATTACK  aims to promote art and artistic traditions for overall personality development of students; by providing the budding them the space and necessary equipment to experiment with and learn diverse art. The society is a hub which promotes the manifestation and portrayal of human creative skills and imagination, in visual form like painting and craftwork. The society utilizes every opportunity to enhance the creativity of our artists and provides them a platform to showcase their talent by organizing different events throughout the year.
  • BIZHIVE Entrepreneurship Society: Bizhive is a student run body which aims to promote entrepreneurship and start-ups spirit at local, national and international level, among student fraternity. The society consists of students who have innovative ideas and a passion to be an entrepreneur. All the activities are planned and executed jointly by society members and faculty in charge. The society firmly believes that universities/colleges shape the minds and skills of their students, therefore, the members of entrepreneurship society take initiative to develop and enlighten by empowering, developing and fostering the young minds in becoming the tomorrow’s leaders and entrepreneurs of tomorrow. Bizhive envisages to helps the students to create a synergistic environment that maximizes networking, engaging conversations and deal flow.
  • Prayas: We Stand For You-The Community Connect Society: Prayas: The Community Connect Society aims to serve as a platform for creating awareness and promoting learning about contemporary social and environmental issues within the context of sustainability and social responsibility. It is composed of a small, yet diverse group of students who share a common interest in sustainable development, charity work, volunteerism, and driving positive change. All activities of the society are planned collaboratively by the society members and the faculty in charge.
