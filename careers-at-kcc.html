Careers @ KCC

Working at KCC Institute

KCC Institute of Technology and Management is looking for people who have the zeal to make it big in the education industry. At KCC Group, exciting careers and smart workplace come with a great sense of belonging. We believe in creating an environment where the most talented, qualified and deserving people wish to work.

At KCC, you have this bright opportunity to build a career you’ll be proud of and help create an exciting new chapter in the education industry.

The Group is committed to a policy of equal opportunity. We ensure that no job applicant or employee receives less favorable treatment on the grounds of sex, race, color, creed, marital status, ethnic origin, sexual orientation, age or disability. We have a code of conduct which every staff member is supposed to follow.

KCC culture emphasizes empowerment of the individuals to make decisions in the best interests of the students.

When you join KCC, you will be introduced to the Group, its background, education programmes and work culture. You will also be personally guided along the way.

To apply for jobs, please send a word document and updated CV along with your latest photograph attached at careers@kccitm.edu.in
