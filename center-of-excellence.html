Center Of Excellence

Training Tie-Up's

The Institute has formed several partnerships and has established various labs to give additional technical training to the students that make the students Industry ready.

Autodesk AAP

KCC Institutes is the authorized academic partners of Autodesk which enables the students to get trained in AutoCAD by specialized trainers. Throughout the program the students undergo various workshops and training module for which they are awarded certificates. Such trainings not only help the students in keeping their curriculum in line with the latest software enhancements from industry but also give them exposure of national level design competitions and other design events. KCC Institutes as an authorized academic partner provides sample projects and course work to help students validate their skills, boost career opportunities and build experience through a project focused learning experience.

Texas Instrument Innovation Centre

KCC Institutes has set up Texas Instrument Innovation Centre in collaboration with Texas Instruments Inc., which is an American technology company and is one of the top ten semi conductor companies’ worldwide. The students get exposure to latest technology and tools and get certificates for the workshops conducted.

The Texas Instruments Innovation centre at KCC Institutes houses five labs as under:

  • Embedded Systems Lab
  • Ultra Low Power Application Lab
  • Analog Lab
  • Internet of Things (IoT) Lab
  • Power Electronics Lab

E-Yantra lab setup Initiative (ELSi)

E- Yantra is an initiative to spread education in embedded systems and Robotics by IIT Bombay sponsored by Ministry of Human Resource Development. E-Yantra lab setup Initiative (eLSi) supports the infrastructures creation at college by providing a platform for training in theory and applications of Robotics there by encouraging the students with hands on project training.

RedHat Academy

RedHat academy setup acts as a centre for enterprise ready talent by outfitting them with RedHat training. The students are given on campus trainings and certification program in RedHat enterprise Linux and RHCSA ( Red hat certified system administrator) which is offered in Technologies like cloud computing, Infrastructure services, IT security and so on. The students not only get an access to courseware and online training sessions by RedHat but also get a free membership signup in RedHat.

Power System Lab

Power system lab provides a robust platform to students for hands on training on power practices like power generation, transmission, distributing smart grids etc. Students get an access to lab manuals, audio and video tutorials along with the chance to attend various practical trainings on live equipments demonstrated by industry experts.

CISCO

Cisco Networking Academy is a global education program that teaches students how to design, build, troubleshoot, and secure computer networks for increased access to career and economic opportunities in communities around the world. Networking Academy provides online courses, interactive tools, and hands-on learning activities to help students prepare for ICT and networking careers in virtually every type of industry. Networking Academy helps students prepare for industry-recognized certifications and entry-level information and communication technology (ICT) careers in virtually every type of industry.

Oracle Academy

To support continuous computer science learning at all levels, the institute in collaboration with Oracle Academy makes sure that the students are offered various Oracle training certifications in Java, database design, SQL and PL/SQL apart from getting a continuous access over the latest study modules.
