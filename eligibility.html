Eligibility

UG Programs (KCC ITM)

Candidate must have passed 10+2 examination with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects along with one of the following subjects: Chemistry / Biotechnology / Biology.

Apply Online

UG Programs (KCC ILHE)

For BBA, BA(JMC) and B.Com(H)

Pass in 12th Class or 10+2 of CBSE or equivalent with a minimum of 50% marks in aggregate and must also have passed English (core or elective or functional) as a subject.

Eligibility Criteria BCA

  • Pass in 12th Class of 10+2 of CBSE or equivalent with a minimum of 50% marks in aggregate
  • Pass in English (core or elective or functional) and Mathematics or Computer Science / Information Practice or Computer Application / Multimedia and Web Technology / Data Management application and Web Application as compulsory subject of the non-vocational stream.

Eligibility Criteria for BBA LLB (H) and BA LLB (H)

  • BA LLB (H): To get admission in GGSIPU colleges for BA LLB (H), candidates must have passed 10+2 with at least 50% marks from a recognized board. They must have English as an optional subject in 12. They must clear CLAT BA LLB entrance exam with valid marks to qualify for admission/counseling. There is no age limit for BA LLB in GGSIPU colleges as per the Bar Council of India. 

 

  • BBA LLB (H): To get admission in GGSIPU colleges for BBA LLB, candidates must have passed 10+2 with at least 50% marks from a recognized board. They must have English as an optional subject in 12. They must clear CLAT 2025 entrance exam with valid marks to qualify for admission/counseling. According to the Bar Council of India, there is no age restriction for BA LLB programs in GGSIPU colleges.

Graduate Programs (KCC ITM)

Candidates should have a Bachelor’s degree from a recognized university in the stream in which they wish to pursue an M.Tech. at KCC. Candidates must have passed B.E/B.Tech. in their respective disciplines with a minimum of 60% in aggregate. SC/ST/PwD candidates.

Is it possible to apply for Graduate courses at KCC ITM with a minimum of 54% in aggregate in the 12th standard? After GATE students are assigned seats, non-GATE candidates are admitted to M.Tech programs based on their qualifying exam scores.

Graduate students from any stream who have at least 50% (or 45% for SC/ST) can apply to KCC ITM's MBA program.
