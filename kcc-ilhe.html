KCC Institute of Legal and Higher Education

KCC Institute of Legal and Higher Education stands out as a premier choice for higher studies, thanks to its exceptional blend of highly qualified faculty, cutting-edge infrastructure, practical curriculum aligned with industry demands, international collaborations, hands-on internships, and strong placement support.

Aspiring students who have completed Class 12 and seek a 3-year undergraduate program in areas like computer applications, business management, commerce and finance, journalism and mass communication, or law can undoubtedly apply to KCC Institute of Legal and Higher Education.

KCC Institute of Legal and Higher Education is a sub-set of KCC Group of Institutes, founded in 2018 under the auspices of the Deepak Gupta Education Trust, Chaired by Sri Deepak Gupta. Located in the vibrant core of Greater Noida, we operate as a fully independent private residential college on a sprawling 40-acre vibrant lush green campus.

KCC ILHE has earned dual "A" grade accreditations from Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University for its sought-after undergraduate programs, including BBA, BCA, B.Com, and BJMC—all approved by AICTE—along with our integrated 5-year dual-degree offerings in BA LLB and BBA LLB, recognized by the Bar Council of India.

KCC ILHE is a symbol of enhancing practical learning and educational ethics.

Ranked among the top private institutes in Delhi NCR, KCC Institute of Legal and Higher Education has received numerous honours and awards in recognition of its outstanding contributions to higher education in Delhi NCR and its ability to shape young people into competent professionals.

Our Training and Placement Cell

The Training & Placement Cell (T&P Cell) at KCC Institute of Legal and Higher Education is essential in connecting students with the business and giving them the direction and assistance they need to start a prosperous career. To facilitate students' successful transfer from academics to the workplace, T&P Cell also assists students in bridging the gap between academia and industry.

Some of the main responsibilities of the KCC ILHE’s T&P Cell include the following:

  • Campus recruitment drives: The T&P Cell is in charge of encouraging businesses to hold campus recruitment drives. This includes liaising with the businesses, setting up interviews, and offering logistical assistance.
  • Giving career advice: The T&P Cell advises students on a range of career options, employment opportunities, and market trends. They also provide training in essential soft skills such as leadership, teamwork, and communication, each vital for thriving in professional environments.
  • Encouraging internships and industry visits: The T&P Cell also organizes internships and industry trips for students, giving them hands-on experience in the field and assisting them in better comprehending the workplace culture.
  • Database maintenance: The T&P Cell is in charge of keeping track of all students and their profiles, as well as a database of employers and their employment requirements. This enables them to align students' interests and abilities with companies' career requirements.
  • Networking with industry experts: The T&P Cell maintains an active network of industry experts and professionals, inviting them to mentor students, give career advice, and give guest lectures.
