B.Tech. in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Course Overview

Apply Online

B. Tech in Artificial Learning and Machine Learning is an undergraduate program offering advanced learning solutions that transmit knowledge of cutting-edge breakthroughs like machine learning, also known as deep learning and artificial intelligence.  

Through the use of cutting-edge machine learning, analytics, and visualization technologies, this specialization aims to empower students to create intelligent machines, software, or apps. The primary objective of B. Tech in artificial intelligence and machine learning is to teach computers to solve problems by using sample data or experience.

Numerous successful machine learning-based applications already exist, such as systems that recognize faces or spoken speech, optimize robot behavior to complete tasks with the least amount of resources, extract knowledge from bioinformatics data, and analyze historical sales data to predict customer behavior (financial management).

To provide a cohesive approach to machine learning issues and solutions, this curriculum examines AI techniques rooted in various domains, such as neural networks, signal processing, control, and data mining.

In India, a B. Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Machine learning graduate’s salary can range from 3.5 Lakhs to 23 Lakhs for those with less than a year to seven years of experience. Estimates of salary are based on 400 recent AIML Engineer salaries.

Numerous professions in artificial intelligence and machine learning are available to students such as robotics engineers, data scientists, machine learning engineers, and more. You can become eligible for these positions with a B. Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.

Lateral Entry

A three-year diploma (finished) in the field of computer science from any accredited university, board, or institute that grants diplomas and has at least 50% of the aggregate marks.

Computer Labs

Every computer in the college has the most recent hardware and software installed. Additionally, every center has a continuous power backup.

  • Programming Lab

  • Projects Lab
  • Chemistry Lab

  • Workshop Lab

 
Eligibility of B. Tech Computer Science Engineering College in Greater Noida, Delhi NCR
Candidate must have passed 10+2 examination with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects along with one of the following subjects: Chemistry / Biotechnology / Biology.
Admission Procedure - BTech CSE College in Greater Noida
Admission to the B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering would be done on the basis of JEE Mains 2026 & 10+2. Management seats are filled directly by the institute by conducting institute level counseling.
NRI Seats
5% of the management seats would be available for NRI candidates. NRI seats would be filled up as per AICTE and University norms i.e. by NRIs who have passed the qualifying examination with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics from an institute situated outside India provided such qualification is considered equivalent to 10+2 Examination by the Association of Indian Universities.
