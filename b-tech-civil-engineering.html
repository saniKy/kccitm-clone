B.Tech Civil Engineering

With Specialisation in: Structural engineering, Project Management

Course Overview

Apply Online

Civil Engineering is a broad field of engineering dealing with the design, planning, construction and maintenance of fixed structures or public works as they are related to earth, water, or civilization and their processes. It is the oldest of all engineering fields.

  1.  
  2.  

 
Eligibility of B.Tech Civil Engineering Colleges in Delhi NCR
Candidate must have passed 10+2 examination with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects along with one of the following subjects: Chemistry / Biotechnology / Biology.
Admission Procedure of Top B.Tech CE College in Delhi NCR
Admission to the B.Tech. Computer Science and Engineering at KCC Institute would be done on the basis of JEE 2026 & 10+2. Management seats are filled directly by the institute by conducting institute level counseling.
NRI Seats
A total of 5% of management quota seats are allocated to NRI applicants. NRI seats would be filled up as per AICTE and University norms i.e. by NRIs who have passed the qualifying examination with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics from an institute situated outside India provided such qualification is considered equivalent to 10+2 Examination by the Association of Indian Universities.

Specialization

AutoCAD is the world’s most widely used 2D and 3D computer aided design (CAD) and drafting software application. AutoCAD is used in most of the industries by architects, project managers, engineers, Graphic designer and many other professionals.

Revit is building information modeling (BIM) software for civil engineers, structural engineers, architects, designers and contractors so capture and analyze design concepts and accurately maintain coordinated design data through documentation and construction.

Software is building information modeling (BIM) software for civil and structural engineers providing industry-specific tools for structural design and analysis.

This is widely used by civil engineers and architects for 3D visualizations of their designs. Though, you will use AutoCAD software for drawings, you can import them into 3d's Max for rendering photo realistic visualization.

Microsoft project is project management software product. It is designed to assist a project manager in developing a plan assigning resources to task, tracking progress, managing the budget and analyzing workloads.

Sports Scholarship

Defence Scholarship

Children of defence personnel's are ofered scholarship to the extent of 10% of fee each semester. The student has to produce card of his parent at the time of admission as a proof of service. The scholarship is also offered to wards of Ex-servicemen.
