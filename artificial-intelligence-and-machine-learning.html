Computer Science and Engineering (Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning)

Course Overview

Apply Online

B Tech in Computer Science and Technology (Spl. in B tech Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning) is an undergraduate programme with advanced learning solutions imparting knowledge of advanced innovations like machine learning, often called deep learning and artificial intelligence.

This B Tech in AI and Machine Learning program prepares graduates to use AI and machine learning technology to solve technical challenges by fusing theoretical knowledge with real-world applications. As one of the best B. Tech Artificial Intelligence colleges affiliated with AKTU and approved by AICTE, our curriculum is designed to cover fields including machine learning (ML), deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision, fundamental computer science engineering courses, and specific courses in artificial intelligence engineering.

Through labs, projects, and internships, the B Tech in AI and Machine Learning program strongly emphasizes practical learning and allows students to build and implement AI algorithms firsthand.

Graduates of our B Tech in AI and Machine Learning program can work in a variety of industries, including technology, healthcare, finance, and the automotive industry, as AI engineers, data scientists, and machine learning engineers, with a lucrative B. Tech Artificial Intelligence salary.

B Tech Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning salary at KCC ITM can range from INR 5-8 LPA while our B Tech Artificial Intelligence fees can cost you only INR 4.8* Lakhs in total. 

 

B Tech in AI and Machine Learning Highlights 

  • Stringent & disciplined academics. 
  • Unique in-depth learning on emerging technologies. 
  • A focus on project, lab, and case study-based learning. 
  • Real-world project experience and guidance from experts in the field 
  • Industry-Academia Collaboration Framework 
  • Quality placements. 
  • Student participation in global competitions. 
  • Getting in touch with an internal incubator that supports a range of start-ups.
  • Affordable B Tech Artificial Intelligence Fees for students from all socio-economic backgrounds.

Program Objectives

Our B Tech in AI and Machine Learning program's goals are as follows:
  • Get the students aware of the core ideas and foundational topics in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.
  • To give students the hands-on knowledge and abilities needed to create and run AI/ML models, algorithms, and systems.
  • To introduce subjects related to the business to get students ready for lucrative careers in AI and ML engineering.
  • To allow students work on real-world projects for enhanced practical exposure on how to handle different challenges.
  • To investigate how AI and ML are multidisciplinary by combining ideas from other disciplines, including computer science, mathematics, psychology, philosophy, and neuroscience.
  • By giving students the chance to take part in faculty-led research projects, we hope to better prepare them for advanced research in AI and ML.
  • To prepare students for jobs with a handsome B Tech Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning salary package.

Data Science is also the future of Artificial Intelligence which has grown exponentially in last few years. This specialization is proposed to enable students to develop smart machines with a cutting-edge combination of AI & Data Science technologies like, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science and Statistics.

 

Why take B Tech CSE with AI and Machine Learning course?

Students enrolled in the B.Tech degree in Computer Science and Engineering (Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning) at KCC ITM are prepared for the technologies of Industry 4.0. One of the major perks of Industry 4.0 is the emergence of Machine learning, which refers to creating systems that can mimic human behaviour by learning from them. Our B.Tech CSE in AI and ML program comes with a range of benefits, including various certification courses and a chance to work abroad.

Students will have the chance to work among the experts through internship projects starting from the second year of their schooling to help them stay ahead in the competition.

The following organizations support students' access to the structured learning path. With this approach, students can gain a thorough grasp of the discipline's many facets and, as their academic careers progress, acquire specific knowledge and skills.

  • Amazon
  • Microsoft
  • Google
  • Infosys
  • Wipro
  • Accenture
  • Cognizant

 
Eligibility of B.Tech Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning from Delhi NCR
Candidate must have passed 10+2 examination with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects along with one of the following subjects: Chemistry / Biotechnology / Biology for admission in B. Tech CSE with Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.
Admission Procedure of B.Tech Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning College in Delhi NCR
Admission to the B.Tech. Computer Science and Engineering would be done on the basis of JEE 2026 & 10+2. Management seats are filled directly by the institute by conducting institute level counseling.
NRI Seats
NRI candidates will be eligible for 5% of the managerial positions. NRI seats would be filled up as per AICTE and University norms i.e. by NRIs who have passed the qualifying examination with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics from an institute situated outside India provided such qualification is considered equivalent to 10+2 Examination by the Association of Indian Universities.
