Vice Chairperson's Message

It gives us immense pleasure as we welcome one and all to the KCC Community. The KCC group has flourished and progressed in India's educational system during the last 60 years, leading the way in creating opportunities for lakhs of students. Whether it is through academic programs or through a bevy of extracurricular activities, KCC's faculty and staff have been known to wholeheartedly dedicate themselves to making a considerable difference in the lives of our students and of those in our community.

We hope you will join us as we work to make a significant impact. KCC Institutes provides the ideal platform for you to learn and understand as much as you can to excel in your field. Don't limit yourself to only a few areas of expertise and prowess. We at KCC help you in expanding your credentials and reaching your true potential.

You will find an institution that has been at the forefront of research activities for the past few decades, as I welcome you all to the school that aspires to excellence in all facets of teaching.

I see a lot of prospects for KCC and its students in the future. Through ongoing curriculum creation and progress, which puts you all at the centre of success, we will continue to transform the academic experience for students.

 

Mrs. Poonam Gupta

Vice Chairperson
