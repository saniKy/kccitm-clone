Our Location

The state of Uttar Pradesh's youngest, most promising, cleanest, greenest city is Greater Noida, located in Delhi NCR. Nothing is lacking in the city thanks to its extensive infrastructure design. The city ranks highly on all needed criteria, including its modern architecture, offices of global corporations, and fresh opportunities for the younger generation. For students, the creation of Knowledge Park is nothing less than a blessing. The Greater Noida Industrial Development Authority has set aside a massive 1400 acres for the construction of Knowledge Park. Numerous universities, colleges, schools, training facilities, and other establishments may be found in Knowledge Park, the educational powerhouse. It is among Delhi NCR's most student-friendly cities.

The strategically located city has many advantages to offer and some of them are:

02 Min Distance from Knowledge Park II Aqua Line Metro Station
25 Minutes away from Delhi and direct metro connectivity from Delhi
50 Minutes away from New Delhi Railway Station.
56 Minutes away from IGI Airport
  • Pollution-free environment
  • 60-105 meter wide road network
  • Construction of Yamuna Expressway to connect Greater Noida to Agra
  • Houses many residential sectors and apartments
  • Houses many huge industrial, multinational and national companies
  • Uninterrupted water supply
  • Well-developed recreational Centre’s, shopping malls, playgrounds etc
  • Various specialized hospitals
  • International Airport coming up at Jewar
  • Coming up with night safari
  • Formula-1 racing track.
  • Developed IT parks, biotech parks, export promotional parks, electronic public library etc
