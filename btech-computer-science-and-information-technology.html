B.Tech. in Computer Science & Information Technology

Course Overview

Apply Online

B. Tech in computer science and information technology (CS & IT) at KCC Institute of Technology and Management covers many foundational topics in Electrical engineering, mathematics, and linguistics. Over the past three decades, computer science and information science have become distinct engineering disciplines after previously being taught as components of engineering or mathematics departments.

B Tech. Computer Science and Information Technology is a full-time, 4-year, undergraduate program designed to produce driven, creative, and enthusiastic graduates who can fill ICT jobs in a variety of industries and who can conceptualize, create, analyse, and implement ICT applications to satisfy contemporary demands.

The most cutting-edge theoretical ideas and practical skills in the field are taught in our B.Tech in Computer Science and Information Technology program. Students who participate in this program gain the critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills necessary for a seamless transition from school to the business world. To broaden their scope of work, students are also taught trans-disciplinary subjects and attitude training.

Algorithm development, analysis, programming languages, software design, computer hardware, e-commerce, business information technology, data analytics, machine learning, block chain technology, augmented virtual reality, mobile application development, the Internet of Things, wireless sensor networks, and web technology are just a few of the fields that fall under the umbrella of computer science and information technology (CS & IT).

Course Curriculum

  • Semester 1 – Multivariable Calculus & Linear Algebra
  • Semester 2 – Engineering Chemistry
  • Semester 3 – Communication Skills
  • Semester 4 – Programming with C
  • Semester 5 – Elements of Mechanical Engineering
  • Semester 6 – IoT and Applications (Innovation)
  • Semester 7 – Design Thinking (Entrepreneurship)
  • Semester 8 – Programming with C Lab

 

Our B.Tech program in CS and IT focuses on high-demand areas, including:

  • Web development
  • Mobile-application development
  • Data analysis
  • Cyber security
  • Network and system computing
  • ICT management
  • Software engineering and development
  • Network management
  • Global leadership

 

Course Duration

The program lasts 4 years and is divided into 8 semesters.

 

Program educational objectives

Following a few years of graduation, computer science and information technology (B.Tech) graduates of KCC Institutes of Technology and Management will be capable of:

  • Seek postsecondary study in the foundational or related fields of information technology and computer science.
  • Pursue a technical profession in the foundational or related fields of information technology and computer science, or launch your own business to boost the economy.
  • Keep learning and adjusting to the rapidly evolving technologies in the foundational or related fields of information technology and computer science.

 

Program Outcomes

After completing the B.Tech (Computer Science and Information Technology) degree successfully at KCC Institute of Technology and Management, graduates will be qualified to:

  • Utilize your understanding of science, math, and engineering principles to solve computer science and information technology problems.
  • Use the fundamentals of mathematics, the natural sciences, and engineering sciences to identify, create, review, and analyse difficult engineering problems to draw well-supported conclusions.
  • Create solutions for challenging engineering issues and system parts or procedures that satisfy requirements while taking into account cultural, socioeconomic, environmental, and public health and safety factors.
  • To provide reliable results, apply research-based knowledge and research techniques, such as experiment design, data analysis and interpretation, and information synthesis.
  • Develop, pick, and use suitable methods, materials, and contemporary IT and engineering tools, such as modelling and prediction, for intricate engineering tasks while being aware of the constraints.
  • Evaluate societal, health, safety, legal, and cultural issues and the corresponding obligations pertinent to professional engineering practice using reasoning based on contextual knowledge.
  • Recognize how professional engineering solutions affect environmental and societal situations, and exhibit your understanding of and commitment to sustainable development.
  • Adhere to professional ethics, duties, and engineering practice norms by putting ethical concepts into practice.
  • Perform well both alone and as a leader or team member in a variety of disciplinary contexts.
  • In the broader context of technological development, acknowledge the necessity of, and possess the readiness and capacity for, independent, lifelong learning.

 

Career Scope

In their offices in India and abroad, numerous IT companies employ a large number of computer science and IT specialists. Students who successfully finish this degree from KCC Institute of Technology and Management can therefore pursue industrial job prospects in the IT and related industries, such as:

  • Information security analyst: By keeping an eye out for security flaws and breaches in their company's network and developing contingency plans in case of an attack, IT security analysts try to stop Cyberattacks.
  • Network architect: Also known as a network engineer, these IT specialists plan and construct communication networks, including intranets, wide area networks (WANs), and local area networks (LANs).
  • Computer support specialist: Support experts assist businesses and people with software-related inquiries by offering guidance and troubleshooting assistance.
  • Database administrators (DBAs): Arrange and store data for industries ranging from shipping corporations to financial institutions using software and programs.
  • System administrators: They are responsible for the daily upkeep and management of a company's network, which includes intranets, LANs, WANs, and other communication systems. Industry-specific compensation varies for this role.

 

Course Fees

The overall cost of B. Tech Computer Science and Information Technology at KCC Institute of Technology and Management is INR 4,80,000. The entire fee can be paid in instalments of INR 60,000 per semester using debit/credit cards, UPI, mobile banking, etc. 

 
Eligibility of B.Tech in Computer Science and Information Technology
Candidate must have passed 10+2 examination with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects along with one of the following subjects: Chemistry / Biotechnology / Biology for admission in B. Tech in Computer Science and Information Technology.
Admission Procedure of B.Tech Computer Science and Information Technology College
Admission to the B.Tech. Computer Science and information Technology would be done on the basis of JEE 2025 & 10+2. Management seats are filled directly by the institute by conducting institute level counseling.
NRI Seats
5% of the management seats would be available for NRI candidates. NRI seats would be filled up as per AICTE and University norms i.e. by NRIs who have passed the qualifying examination with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics from an institute situated outside India provided such qualification is considered equivalent to 10+2 Examination by the Association of Indian Universities.
