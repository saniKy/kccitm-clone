Computer Science and Engineering (Data Science)

Course Overview

Apply Online

B. Tech in Computer Engineering with a focus on Data Science (DS) is a relatively young and rapidly expanding area that consists of a collection of methods and instruments for deriving valuable information from data. The curriculum includes Data Science as an interdisciplinary, problem-solving-focused course that teaches students how to apply scientific methods to real-world issues.

Data Science is the conceptual framework which is used in Analytics, this would include statistics, computational physics, algebra, CS and all allied subjects.
Qualifications for Delhi NCR's B.Tech in Data Science
The candidate must have passed the 10+2 exam with one of the following topics in addition to the required subjects of mathematics and physics: Biology, Chemistry, and Biotechnology.
Admission Procedure of B.Tech Data Science College in Delhi NCR
Admission to the B.Tech. Computer Science and Engineering at KCC ITM would be done on the basis of JEE 2026 & 10+2. Management seats are filled directly by the institute by conducting institute level counseling.
NRI Seats
NRI candidates will be eligible for 5% of the managerial positions. NRI seats would be filled up as per AICTE and University norms i.e. by NRIs who have passed the qualifying examination with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics from an institute situated outside India provided such qualification is considered equivalent to 10+2 Examination by the Association of Indian Universities.

  • Economically Weaker Section Scholarship
  • Minority (MOMA) Scholarship
  • J&K Scholarships
  • North Eastern Region Scholarships
  • Scholarships for the Students with Disabilities
  • Scholarship For Defence Families – The Institute Grants 10% waiver in Tuition Fee
  • Pragati – Scholarship for Girl Students

Complete details about the scholarships by Centre & State Government can be obtained from the admission office of the institute or social welfare officer of the concerned State Government.

 

Scholarship based on class XIIth score
  • 90% and above in eligiblity subjects - 100% of semester fee
  • 80%-89% in eligiblity subjects - 50% of semester fee.
  • 75%-79% in eligiblity subjects - 25% of semester fee
  • 70%-74% in eligiblity subjects - 15% of semester fee

Sports Scholarship
  • Zonal Zonal Level Player : 5% of fee each year
  • State State Level Player : 15% of fee each year
  • National National Level Player : 25% of fee each year
Defence Scholarship

Children of defence personnel's are ofered scholarship to the extent of 10% of fee each semester. The student has to produce card of his parent at the time of admission as a proof of service. The scholarship is also offered to wards of Ex-servicemen.
