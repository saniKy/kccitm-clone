B.Tech. in Computer Science & Engineering

With Specialisation in : Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Cloud Computing & Networking, Java Programming & Oracle, Hadoop & Big Data, Web and Mobile Development

Course Overview

Apply Online

A B.Tech in Computer Science Engineering stands out as a vibrant, adaptable program brimming with career prospects for the fast-paced digital natives of today. In the initial year, students dive into foundational topics like computer networks, hardware essentials, and practical applications. By the final year, they've mastered the intricate details that define the field.

Today's digital landscape thrives on computer-powered innovations, shaping every aspect of our connected lives. Picture yourself as a fresh B.Tech CSE graduate, eager to dive deep into this tech-driven realm. Greater Noida's top B.Tech CSE colleges offer an ideal launchpad, equipping you with the skills and expertise to thrive in the competitive tech sector.

Pursuing a B. Tech in Computer Science Engineering goes beyond routine academics—it's a thrilling journey packed with transformative possibilities. Graduates from B.Tech CSE colleges in Greater Noida consistently rave about their enriching experiences and the doors it opens

 
 

 

Key Elements

Modern Curriculum

Impart knowledge in the main fields of information technology, including cloud computing, databases, networking, artificial intelligence, web development, and data analytics.

Professionalism

To improve personality and employability, the course covers areas like communication, analysis, and soft skills in addition to the main curriculum.

Industry Immersion

Industry-partnered trainings, hands-on projects, and sessions with guest experts immerse students in authentic, real-world scenarios.

Projects

Technical and presentation abilities are intended to be improved through seminars and project-driven learning.

Industrial Visits

For students, college organizes frequent field trips to leading industries, giving students hands-on exposure to cutting-edge innovations and real-time problem-solving scenarios.

Multidisciplinary Electives

Students have the opportunity to specialize in multidisciplinary fields of interest, such as Ai and ML, Data Science, IoT, Blockchain, Cybersecurity, and more.

Software Proficiency

21st-century digital tools are incorporated into the curriculum for data analytics, web design, networking, mobile app development, and databases.

Industrial Contribution

The course curriculum is capable of imparting skills based on feedback from prestigious academic and industry groups.

Creative Teaching Methods

Demo kits are used in conjunction with animations, simulations, audio-visual aids, and demonstrations to provide an interactive teaching style.

Comprehensive Growth

Sports, cultural events, and technical competitions all contribute to students' overall growth.
Eligibility of B. Tech Computer Science Engineering College in Greater Noida, Delhi NCR
Candidate must have passed 10+2 examination with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects along with one of the following subjects: Chemistry / Biotechnology / Biology.
Admission Procedure - BTech CSE College in Greater Noida
Admission to the B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering would be done on the basis of JEE Mains 2026 & 10+2. The institute handles management seat admissions entirely on its own through institute level counseling sessions.
NRI Seats
Approximately 5% of management quota seats are reserved for NRI candidates, with admissions conducted strictly according to AICTE guidelines and university regulations for NRIs who have passed the qualifying examination with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics from an institute situated outside India provided such qualification is considered equivalent to 10+2 Examination by the Association of Indian Universities.

Specialization

AutoCAD stands out as the leading software for 2D and 3D computer-aided design (CAD) and drafting across the globe. Professionals like architects, engineers, project managers, graphic designers, and others in various industries rely on it daily for precise and efficient work.

Crafting dynamic web applications represents a key aspect of web development. These are interactive platforms, such as social media giants like Facebook or online retail hubs like Amazon, where users engage seamlessly with real-time features, databases, and user-generated content.

On the other hand, mobile app development focuses on building software optimized for portable devices like smartphones and tablets. Developers leverage the device's native hardware—such as cameras, GPS, and sensors—along with platform-specific tools to deliver smooth, feature-rich experiences tailored to mobile constraints.

Blockchain functions as a collaborative, publicly accessible digital record book, decentralized and replicated across countless computers worldwide. It captures transactions with robust security, making it nearly impossible to alter past entries without recalibrating every subsequent block and securing approval from most network participants. Commonly known as a distributed ledger technology (DLT), it supports trustworthy, tamper-proof documentation of interactions between untrusted parties.

By dispersing and synchronizing information across diverse geographies, entities, or systems—free from any central overseer or repository—this approach earns its reputation as a decentralized registry for digital value.

 

Cryptocurrency represents a digital form of money secured through advanced cryptographic techniques and powered by a shared digital ledger known as blockchain. This innovative setup allows creators to build financial systems that prioritize security, user privacy, transparency in transactions, and operation without a central authority.

Often referred to as digital currencies, virtual assets, or alternative money, cryptocurrencies enable direct peer-to-peer transfers using pairs of public and private keys for robust protection. Unlike conventional banking or centralized payment processors, they function across vast, distributed networks that no single entity controls.

Artificial Intelligence describes the advanced abilities machines demonstrate, distinguishing them from the natural smarts of humans and animals. It enables devices or systems to sense their surroundings and make decisions that boost their odds of reaching set objectives.

The Internet of Things (IOT) refers to an interconnected ecosystem of everyday physical items—like vehicles, household gadgets, appliances, and other devices—that are equipped with sensors, software, electronics, and network capabilities. This setup allows them to communicate seamlessly and share data in real time.

IOT represents the cutting edge of technological advancement, as global adoption accelerates and promises to reshape daily life in groundbreaking ways in the years ahead.

Cloud computing delivers essential IT resources—such as servers, data storage, databases, networking tools, software applications, and analytics—directly through the internet, often called "the cloud." Service providers in this space, known as cloud vendors, charge users based on consumption, much like paying a utility bill for water or power. This model marks a major departure from outdated approaches to managing business IT infrastructure. Top players dominating the global market include Amazon Web Services (AWS), Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud Platform.
